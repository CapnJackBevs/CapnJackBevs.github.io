I'm Willow a second-year PhD student at Edinburgh under Clark Barwick. I study ∞-categorical homotopy theory and its applications - from Exodromy and Pyknotic (or "condensed") mathematics, to spectra, cohomology and chromatic homotopy theory.

My mathematical interests include, but are not limited to, the following

  • ∞-categories, ∞-topoi and ∞-structures
  • Exodromy and Pyknotic/Condensed mathematics
  • Cohomology and Spectra; E∞-rings
  • Chromatic homotopy theory
On the non-mathematical side I'm an avid baker, a climber, and a non-binary Quaker! In my past I've been a fire-eater and a juggler in various circus troupes and an assitant (recreational) pirate. In 2016 I ran away from the circus to pursue an undergraduate degree in mathematics and decided that the old gigs' ship had sailed. So here I am, washed ashore the rocky beaches of the algebro-topological world and it has been a considerable effort to stay afloat, but I'm glad that I have.
