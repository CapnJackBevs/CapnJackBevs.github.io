I'm a second-year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh under Clark Barwick. I study ∞-categorical homotopy theory

On the non-mathematical side I'm an avid baker and knitter. In my past I've been a fire-eater and a juggler in various circus troupes and an assitant (recreational) pirate. In 2016 I ran away from the circus to pursue an undergraduate degree in mathematics. So here I am, washed ashore the rocky beaches of homotopy theory.
